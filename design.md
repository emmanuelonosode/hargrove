### Colour rules
- **Primary ad background options:** Dark Navy `#0B1F3A` or Brand Blue `#1A56DB` or clean White `#FFFFFF`
- **Text on dark backgrounds:** Always white `#FFFFFF` — never grey
- **CTA buttons:** Brand Blue `#1A56DB` on white backgrounds · White button on dark/blue backgrounds
- **Never use:** Red (except errors), orange, purple, or yellow as primary colours
- **Gradient (hero overlays):** `linear-gradient(to bottom, rgba(11,31,58,0.55), rgba(11,31,58,0.96))`
- **Dark overlay on photos:** `#0B1F3A` at 55–85% opacity so white text stays readable

## 3. Typography

### Fonts
| Role | Font | Weight | Size guidance |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / Hero headline** | Playfair Display | Bold 700 / Black 900 | 40px+ only |
| **Section heading** | DM Sans | Bold 700–800 | 28–40px |
| **Subheading** | DM Sans | SemiBold 600 | 18–26px |
| **Body copy** | DM Sans | Regular 400 | 14–16px, line-height 1.6 |
| **Eyebrow label** | DM Sans | SemiBold 600 | 10–12px, ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.3–0.4em |
| **CTA button text** | DM Sans | Bold 700 | 13–15px |

**Google Fonts import:**
```
https://fonts.google.com/share?selection.family=Playfair+Display:wght@700;900|DM+Sans:wght@400;500;600;700;800
```

**Rule:** Playfair Display is *only* for large hero moments (the main headline). All other text uses DM Sans.

### Headline formulas
- Serif headline + Sans subheading = Premium, trustworthy feel (use on flyers and social)
- All-sans bold + eyebrow label = Clean, direct (use on digital ads and banners)

## 6. Brand Voice — Do & Don't

### ✅ DO say
- "No hidden fees"
- "What you see is what you pay"
- "24-hour decisions"
- "Apply in 10 minutes"
- "Real families, honest prices"
- "We work for you, not commissions"
- "No games, no runaround"
- "Affordable" (use freely — it's our positioning)
- "Kids welcome · Pets welcome" (where applicable)
- Specific numbers: "3-bed from $1,400/mo" — never vague

### ❌ DON'T say
- "Luxury" / "Premium" / "Exclusive" (not our market)
- "World-class" / "Revolutionary" / "Best-in-class"
- "Starting from" without a real price attached
- "Subject to change"
- "Processing fee" / "Admin fee" (we don't charge them — ever)
- Passive or corporate-speak: "We strive to deliver" → say "We deliver"
- Vague promises: "Fast approvals" → say "24-hour decisions"

### Tone in one line
**Direct, warm, and specific.** Like a trusted friend who knows real estate.

## 10. Photo & Image Style

### What to show
- Real families in real homes — not models in penthouses
- Bright, welcoming interiors with natural light
- Front doors, driveways, family moments: unpacking, relaxing in living rooms, kids in backyard
- Clean but lived-in spaces — not sterile staged shoots
- Diverse families (reflect our actual customer base)
- Affordable-looking spaces: functional kitchens, cosy bedrooms, practical bathrooms

### Mood
**Warm · Hopeful · Attainable.**  
Natural light. Slightly warm colour temperature. Feels like a Sunday morning.

### What to avoid
- Luxury penthouses, marble countertops, rooftop pools
- All-white minimalist loft apartments
- Empty rooms with no people
- Stock photo faces that look too polished or staged
- Anything that looks expensive or aspirational — we are affordable, show it

### Recommended photo overlay for ads
When placing text over a photo, apply a dark navy gradient:
```
Background: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(11,31,58,0.82) 0%, rgba(11,31,58,0.55) 100%)
```
This keeps the navy brand feel while keeping text readable.

### Free stock sources
- **Unsplash.com** — search: "family home interior", "affordable apartment", "house rental", "moving in", "cosy living room"
- **Pexels.com** — search: "rental home", "family house", "affordable housing"
- **Pixabay.com** — search: "house exterior", "family moving", "home key"

## 16. Canva Quick-Start

1. Open Canva → Create design → enter size from **Section 9**
2. Set background to `#0B1F3A` (Dark Navy) or upload a photo with overlay
3. Upload logo (`/public/logo.svg` exported as PNG from the website)
4. Add Playfair Display headline from Google Fonts
5. Add DM Sans body/subheading
6. Pull copy from **Section 5** (Key Messages) or **Section 12** (Copy Bank)
7. Add 1–2 trust signals from **Section 7**
8. CTA button: `#1A56DB` background, white text, no border radius (sharp corners = brand style)
9. Export: PDF Print (for flyers) or PNG (for digital ads)
