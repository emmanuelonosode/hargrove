## Brand Voice

**Tone**: Honest, direct, warm, no-nonsense. We talk like a trusted friend who happens to know everything about rentals.

**Do say**:
- "No hidden fees" / "What you see is what you pay"
- "24-hour decisions" / "Apply in 10 minutes"
- "Real families, honest prices"
- "Comfortable living, within your budget"
- "We work for you, not commissions"
- "No games, no runaround"

**Don't say**:
- Luxury, premium, exclusive (we are affordable)
- "World-class", "revolutionary", "cutting-edge"
- Vague claims without numbers
- Anything that implies long wait times

## Format Instructions

When generating a marketing asset, always output:

### 1. COPY
Full text ready to paste — headline, body, CTA. Optimised for the platform/format.

### 2. CREATIVE BRIEF
- **Format & dimensions**: e.g. "Facebook Feed 1080×1080"
- **Background**: colour or image description
- **Visual elements**: what to show (photo type, overlays, logo placement)
- **Text placement**: where headline/body/CTA sit
- **Font suggestions**: which brand fonts at what size/weight
- **Colour palette**: which brand colours to use

### 3. VARIANTS (optional)
2–3 copy variations to A/B test if relevant.
